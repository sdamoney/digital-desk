The Person Behind the Desk

Hi! My name is Shrikant Damani.

Allow me to welcome you to my humble online abode. Since I spend most of my waking hours here, my desk is probably the most honest reflection of me you're likely to find.

I'd read somewhere that first impressions are made in about 12 seconds. The average adult reading speed is 200-250 words per minute, which means I have about 48 words to make a good impression here. This desk is my shortcut. Oh well. Batman.

I am a certified Chartered Accountant with an MBA in strategic marketing from MICA. In other words, I can do the math needed in the first paragraph with ease, while also arranging the items on this desk in a way that is, hopefully, a little interesting.

This desk is a labor of love, and the result of an almost compulsive need to arrange my thoughts visually. It's an online journal told through the objects I keep around. You'll find a mix of the mindful and the mindless here. There will, invariably, be brief, meandering segues wherein things will get heavy, probably sitting right next to a bad pun. You have been warned.

Each item is a different part of my story, a different corner of my mind. I'm here to document, not just to create.

So please, feel free to look around. Click on things. Be curious. Thanks for stopping by.

My Journey

2024 - Present

91Ninjas

Brand Lead

"If you know exactly what you want to be in life, you will invariably become it; that is your punishment." – Oscar Wilde. I knew pretty early on that I wanted to become a Chartered Accountant, so I became one. Then I realised that I really wanted to become a marketer. So, I did an MBA, made the switch, and became a marketer. But for the past year or so, I had no idea what my career path would look like. I took a break after my previous job to regroup and allowed curiosity to guide me—and that journey has brought me to 91Ninjas. I believe now, more than ever, that curiosity is a better compass than certainty. I'm thrilled to share that I've joined 91Ninjas, where I'll be leading the brand team. I can’t wait to see what the future holds for me—and, honestly, I love not knowing.

2022 - 2024

Scalenut

Manager - Growth

'Never stop learning' is the mantra that I have been embodying in my time at Scalenut. I have had the opportunity to learn, first hand, how a SaaS product is built, marketed, and improved. I have learned how to write engaging copy, learned the first principles of website designing, prompt engineering, and believe it or not - coding! Yes, this Chartered accountant turned marketeer turned prompt engineer can debug code. I can't quite code yet but never say never!

2021 - 2022

Future Consumer Limited

Assistant Manager - Marketing & Branding

'Nothing is permanent' is the lesson that I learned at FCL. Not your highs/lows, your job, or even your company. At FCL, I managed the strategy and development for brands such as Karmiq, Fresh & Pure, Ektaa, and Desi Atta Company. It was an incredibly enriching experience that was abruptly cut short due to an unprecedented force being applied by the invisible hand.

2021

Vishvaraj Environment Pvt Ltd

Manager - Chairman & Managing Director's Office

After a bitter-sweet COVID-19 infested graduation - it was time to get back to the corporate world, albeit in a different department. At Vishvaraj, I was tasked with the formulation of a PR strategy focused on revamping the digital presence of the company along with providing strategic counsel to the C-suite for new business opportunities and potential investments.

2019

Happily Unmarried Pvt Ltd (Ustraa)

Marketing Intern

It was time to marry the theory I had studied at my first year of MBA with the live environment of working for a company. At Ustraa, I had the chance to conduct primary research in order to garner consumer insights and to study consumer behavior. I also had the opportunity to conduct a feasibility study for a potential product and recommend future course of action for the same.

2018 - 2019

TDKR Technologies Pvt Ltd (a.k.a. Kasa Kai Mumbai)

Marketing Associate

After a rewarding experience at an MNC, it was time, in my career, to experience the world of start-ups. At Kasa Kai Mumbai, I got the opportunity to conceptualise and execute numerous cultural events across the city - ranging from sports events to exclusive Game of Thrones screenings.

2016 - 2018

Morgan Stanley

Associate

I had the privilege of experiencing how a multinational corporation operates and was one of the Risk Managers for MS's proprietary hedge fund platform known as 'Fundlogic SAS'. Incidentally, I was exposed to the marketing/business side of things for the first time which absolutely enthralled me and defined my career path going ahead.

2013 - 2016

Karnavat & Co

Article Associate

I had the opportunity to work with companies across various sectors including banking, manufacturing, retail, service providers etc. wherein I conducted audits (Internal, Statutory) based on comprehensive audit plans.
